About Me

Hi, I'm Harshit, a passionate filmmaker dedicated to telling compelling stories through the lens. My journey in filmmaking is driven by a desire to explore human emotions, societal themes, and the intersection of technology and humanity.

From documentaries that capture raw, personal journeys to narrative films that challenge perceptions, each project is an opportunity to create something meaningful. My work has been recognized at various platforms, including being shortlisted for WAVES 2025 Young Filmmaker Challenge.

I believe in the power of visual storytelling to inspire change, evoke emotions, and connect people across boundaries.
